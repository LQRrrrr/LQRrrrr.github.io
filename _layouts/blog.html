A Detailed Summary of Life

Why wait to start my new life? Some birds are not meant to be caged, their feathers are just too bright.

  • Nov 2001: The Miracle of Birth

    Patient: Is it a boy or a girl?

    Obstetrician: Now, I think I am a girl.

  • Sep 2016 to Jul 2019: Studied at No.7 High School

    Interested in Biology and Physics. Won the third prize in China High School Biology Olympiad and the second prize in China High School Physics Olympiad in Sichuan Province without any specific training.

  • Jul 2018: First GaoKao and First Failure

    Still grateful to the opportunity offered by University of Science and Technology, China . Ranked 5000+ in Sichuan Province but did not pass the test for gifted young held by USTC. Realized that I might not be goot at physics and math.

  • Sep 2019: Entered into Renmin University of China (RUC) with major in statistics

    A totally new journey with amazing new subject regarding to randomness and probability.

  • Jan 2020: Second failure: Got 64 points in Higher Algebra I Final Exam

    Finally got 80 points, the square root of 64 times 10. Depressed and started to think about transferring to another major.

  • Jan 2020: The Outbreak of Covid-19

    Historical moment. Gained 4 kilograms because of laziness and eating (the maximum weight is 68 kg). Depressed because of lockdown. Realized that life is so transient.

  • May 2020 to Aug 2020: Lose Weight by Punction Therapy And Use OK Glass to Cure Eyesight

    Lost 12 kilograms. Had 5.0 eyesight during daytime. Felt the world is much better.

  • Feb 2021: Third Failure: Be a Research Assistant For a Postdoc Doing Much Tedious Work Without Any Payment

    Quitted at Aug 2021. Not all people are friendly and helpful. Hoped he cannot get tenure track. Realized people have to do something that is meaning and they are interested in. Also, payment is the least reward.

  • May 2021 to Aug 2021: Research At LISA Lab Hosted by Prof. Xu at RUC

    Handled my first research project independently. Much effort was paied into it. My research interest changed to Information System.

  • Jul 2021 to Aug 2021: Summer School

    Attended summer school held by RUC and statistical center, Peking university. Great thanks to Prof. Weijie Su and Prof. Yuling Jiao's introduction to statistical learning and high dimensional statistics, I started to think about going further in statistics.

  • Sep 2021 to Jan 2021: Exchange Student at the University of Hong Kong

    Became a research assistant for Prof. Tuan and Prof. Ivy Dang. Became a research assistant for Prof. Dan Yang and Prof. Haipeng Shen. The first group focuses on quantitative marketing and information system. The second group focuses on statistics. They want to solve statistical genetics problems with unique dataset from a hospital. Although this project fails finally, my research interest changes to statistical genetics/biostatistics.

  • Dec 2021 - June 2022: Undergraduate Research Program at RUC

    I am exteremely fortunate to be guided by Dr. Jingshen Wang. She is an excellent and really nice advisor. I learned to be as rigorous as her in research.

  • Feb 2022 -present: Started my first research project in the U.S.

    I am exteremely fortunate to be guided by Dr. Jingshen Wang. She is an excellent and really nice advisor. I learned to be as rigorous as her in research.
